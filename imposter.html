FAQ
Who will I interact with during the course?
Our program typically attracts incredible individuals, mainly remote professionals from various fields.
When do the practical lessons take place?
At the beginning of each week, you'll complete a brief questionnaire about your availability. We'll then pair you with a partner with compatible time slots.
How many practical lessons will I have per week?
We offer 7 lessons in total: three in the first week and two each in the subsequent weeks.
What is the bonus lesson about?
For participants actively seeking employment, we provide an additional intensive session that requires more commitment than regular classes.
Can I skip lessons if necessary?
We highly discourage missing lessons. However, in case of unavoidable circumstances, we are committed to assisting you in catching up with any missed content.
