FAQ
How does the matching process work?

We handpick pairs based on their business interests, personal details, and areas they wish to explore or improve.

How long do the meetings last?

Meetings typically last between 45 to 60 minutes. However, some participants find themselves engaged in conversations that extend up to 3 hours.

Who will I be meeting with?

You'll be meeting with entrepreneurs just like yourself. One common trait among all participants is an openness to new experiences and a readiness for honest conversations.

Where do the meetings take place?

All meetings are conducted online. You'll connect at a location and time that's mutually convenient for both participants.

What if I can't attend a meeting next week?

No worries! At the end of each week, we'll check in to see if you're available for a meeting the following week. If you're unable to participate or simply not interested at that time, you can opt out. Just like a gym membership, even if you're not actively participating, your membership remains valid.
