FAQ
How do I get started with Soft Skills Hub?

Begin by choosing a course from our diverse offerings. Once you've signed up, you can start learning immediately.

How does it work?

Each of our courses combines theory, an AI coach, and 1-to-1 practice sessions to provide a comprehensive learning experience.

How much time will I need to spend on a course?

Typically, you should allocate around 25 minutes per day for course learning. Plus, our 1-to-1 practice sessions usually last around 60 minutes.

Are these self-paced courses, communities, or cohorts?

At their core, our courses are self-paced. You'll receive one lesson per day in your mailbox for 14 days after you sign up. However, you also have the option to join our vibrant community and get access to an AI coach for personalized guidance and support.

How long does a course last?

Each course runs for 14 days, with one lesson delivered to your mailbox daily.

What if the course isn't what I expected?

We prioritize your satisfaction. If you're not pleased even after the free trial (4 lessons), you can cancel and receive a full refund.

What if I have questions about a course?

Don't hesitate to email your questions, and we'll respond within 24 hours.

What if I want to talk personally?

You're welcome to book a call. We're always happy to speak with our learners.

How does Soft Skills Hub ensure a safe learning environment?

Our community guidelines and a dedicated team work tirelessly to maintain an atmosphere of respect and safety.

How often should I practice what I've learned?

Regular practice is key. We recommend participating in our 1-to-1 sessions a few times a week for optimal learning.

Are these courses suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! Our platform caters to everyone, from beginners to those looking to refine their existing skills.
